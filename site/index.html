Claim fee-

Claiming locks 1 BCC inside the contract and lifts this Comrade into a standalone ERC-721 you can trade on any NFT marketplace. The claim fee is paid in ETH to the treasury.

Unclaiming burns this permanent ERC-721 forever (it can never be reissued at this id), releases the locked BCC back to your wallet, and mints a fresh in-404 Comrade with the same seed. The unclaim fee is paid in ETH to the treasury.
