uPEG — seed predictor (read-only)

No wallet, no swap, no gas. Reads the hook's _randomSeed + _randomCount + chain state, simulates the keccak forward, decodes traits.
Caveat: the seed for YOUR mint depends on the prevrandao of the block your tx mines in. Public users can't predict that. This shows what you'd get if your tx landed in the current block right now.
